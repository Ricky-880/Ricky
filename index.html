Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

GlobalCross E-commerce ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our services.

2. Data We Collect

We collect the following types of data:

  • Order information (order ID, products, shipping address)
  • Customer service messages and inquiries
  • Website analytics data (with consent)
  • User profile information (with consent)

3. How We Use Data

We use this data solely for:

  • Processing and fulfilling orders
  • Providing customer support
  • Business analytics and optimization
  • Improving our services
  • Compliance with legal requirements

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Data encryption (AES-256)
  • Secure cloud servers (AWS/Azure)
  • Access controls and multi-factor authentication
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Employee training on data protection

5. Data Sharing

We DO NOT sell or share your data with third parties except:

  • Shipping partners (for order fulfillment)
  • Payment processors (for transactions)
  • Legal requirements (if required by law)
  • Service providers (under strict confidentiality agreements)

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request data correction or deletion
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • File complaints with relevant authorities

7. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary:

  • Order data: 3 years (legal requirement)
  • Analytics data: 1 year
  • Customer messages: 6 months
  • Marketing data: Until opt-out

8. Compliance

We comply with:

  • GDPR (European Union General Data Protection Regulation)
  • CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act)
  • Local data protection laws in all operating regions
