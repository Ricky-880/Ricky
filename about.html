Our Story

GlobalCross E-commerce was founded with a simple mission: to bridge the gap between quality products and global consumers through the power of social commerce.

Starting as a small operation, we have grown into a trusted TikTok Shop partner, serving customers across Southeast Asia, North America, and Europe. Our success is built on three pillars: quality products, exceptional service, and data-driven optimization.

Our Mission & Values

🎯 Our Mission

To provide exceptional shopping experiences through TikTok Shop, connecting customers with quality products while maintaining the highest standards of service and integrity.

👁️ Our Vision

To become a leading cross-border e-commerce company, recognized for innovation, customer satisfaction, and sustainable business practices.

💎 Our Values

  • Teamwork & Collaboration
